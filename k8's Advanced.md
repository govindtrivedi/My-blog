### 🚀 What is Orchestration in Kubernetes?

**Orchestration in Kubernetes** means **automatically managing the lifecycle of containers and applications across a cluster of machines**.

### 🔧 How it works in Kubernetes

Kubernetes acts like a **manager** that handles everything for your application:

*   📦 **Deployment** → Runs your containers (Pods)
    
*   📈 **Scaling** → Increases/decreases instances based on load
    
*   🔁 **Self-healing** → Restarts failed containers automatically
    
*   🌐 **Networking** → Connects services inside/outside cluster
    
*   ⚖️ **Load balancing** → Distributes traffic evenly
    
*   🔄 **Rolling updates** → Updates apps without downtime
